New Policies

Electronic Mail
Please be advised that, until further notice, the Division of Tax Appeals/Tax Appeals Tribunal will no longer provide parties with courtesy copies of Determinations, Decisions or Orders via electronic mail on the date that they are issued.

Small Claims
The New York State Tax Appeals Tribunal (Tribunal) has adopted a policy that determinations issued by the Small Claims Unit of the Division of Tax Appeals (DTA) will no longer be published in any manner.

Hearing Unit
The Tax Appeals Tribunal has instituted a new procedure for the handling of all matters pending before the Division of Tax Appeals' Hearing Unit. Effective June 18, 2007, upon receipt of the Answer in each matter, an Administrative Law Judge (ALJ) will immediately be assigned to preside over that matter.
